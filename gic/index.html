• zplanewave_test - Test of zplanewave.m, which computes the 1-D surface impedance for a plane wave incident on and infinite half space.
  • zplanewave_demo - Exact impedance, phase, and impulse response for various 1-D conductivity profiles. See also Ari's code and NERC.
  • transferfn_demo - Comparison between a direct (no smoothing) frequency domain method and a time domain method for estimating a causal and absolutely integrable transfer function.
  • transferfn_demo2 - Comparison of time and frequency domain approaches to estimating the transfer function for an infinite half-plane (which is causal and not absolutely integrable). Shows limitations in time domain method and appearance of acausal terms in frequency domain due to DFT approximation.
  • transferfn_demo3 - Comparison between time domain and commonly used frequency domain methods for estimating transfer functions for use in prediction. Demonstrates use of functions transferfnFD and transferfnTD for computing transfer functions.
